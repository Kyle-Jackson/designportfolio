Sound Surround is an innovative music playing app that allows users to wirelessly sync their favourite music with the rooms in their house. Users can choose which playlists play in which rooms and as they walk around the house and change rooms, so does the music.

Using the remote in the app, the user will be able to control what playlists are being played in which rooms of the house.

On this screen users can view all of the songs in the playlist. From this screen, users can also add songs to playlist by pressing the plus button in the top right corner of the screen.
