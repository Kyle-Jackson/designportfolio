Big League Bat App Design

Big League Bat is an app that uses wearable technology to provide the user with data regarding their swing.

You can also select the averages tab in the app to see the average of all of the data as well as the average path your bat took when you swung.

Big League Bat tracks your history in the calendar tab so you can see which days you used the app.

Using the calendar tab, you can go in your history and select a day you used the app and see the data from a specific swing.
